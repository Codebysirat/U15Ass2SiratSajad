About Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum

We are passionate about fostering the Slovak language, culture, and traditions within the vibrant South Manchester community.

Our Mission

Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum is dedicated to connecting Slovak families and individuals by providing educational opportunities, celebrating cultural heritage, and fostering community spirit.

Our Vision

To be a hub for cultural preservation and growth, where Slovak traditions thrive and future generations stay connected to their roots.

Our Values

  • Community: Building strong and supportive connections.
  • Education: Empowering individuals through learning.
  • Culture: Celebrating and preserving Slovak heritage.
  • Inclusion: Welcoming people from all backgrounds to share and appreciate Slovak traditions.
