Our Courses

Explore our wide range of courses designed to educate, inspire, and connect the community. We offer classes for all age groups and skill levels!

Featured Courses

  • Slovak Language Classes: Learn to speak, read, and write in Slovak with our experienced instructors.
  • Cultural Workshops: Dive into Slovak traditions, from folk dance to cooking authentic recipes.
  • Creative Arts for Kids: Encourage your child’s creativity through fun and engaging activities.
  • History and Heritage: Discover the rich history of Slovakia and its cultural landmarks.

How to Enroll

Joining our courses is easy! Simply select the course that interests you, and register through our Membership Page. For further inquiries, feel free to contact us via email or phone.
