About Us

We are a dedicated team of volunteers committed to uniting and empowering the Slovak community in South Manchester and its surrounding areas. Our mission is to provide education and cultural activities that help keep Slovak traditions alive. We offer a variety of workshops, cultural events, and educational programs for both children and adults who wish to join and support our community.

Membership Sign-Up

Becoming a member of our charity is a great way to get involved and support our mission. As a member, you will receive regular updates about our events, workshops, and other activities. You will also have the opportunity to participate in our programs and contribute to the growth of our community.
